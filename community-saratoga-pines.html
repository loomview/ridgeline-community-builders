Overview

Open space, right outside your door

Saratoga Pines is a new community of ranch and two-story homes in Wilton, wrapped by more than 300 acres of preserved open space. It is the kind of place where you can walk for miles, meet your neighbors at the clubhouse, and still be minutes from everything Saratoga County offers.

Location & Lifestyle

Minutes from Saratoga Springs

Wilton puts you close to the dining, shopping, and culture of downtown Saratoga Springs while keeping a quieter, more open feel at home. Families are served by well-regarded local public schools, and major commuter routes are an easy reach. Weekends here lean outdoors: trails, courts, and a lot of fresh air.
