Our Process

Five phases, zero guesswork

1

Discovery

We start by listening. You tell us how you live, what you need, and where you want to be, and we match the right community, homesite, and floor plan to your budget and timeline.

2

Design

Next we make it yours. You select finishes, options, and upgrades with our design team, so the home reflects your taste before a single wall goes up.

3

Pre-Construction

Before we break ground, we lock in your plans, permits, and selections and walk you through exactly what to expect. No surprises, clear milestones, one point of contact.

4

Build

This is where it comes to life. Our team builds your home to spec with regular updates and quality checks at every stage, so you always know where things stand.

5

One-Year Warranty Walkthrough

We don't disappear at closing. Around the one-year mark we walk the home with you, address anything that needs attention, and make sure it is performing the way it should.
